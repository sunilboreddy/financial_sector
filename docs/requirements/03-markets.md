# Requirement: Markets & venues module

**What:** Explain exchange vs OTC trading, order book mechanics, the full trade lifecycle from order to settlement, and the categories of market data (tick, OHLCV, reference data).

**Why:** Most finance data pipelines exist to capture, reconcile, or report on some stage of the trade lifecycle. The learner needs to know where in that lifecycle a given dataset/feed sits, and what "market data" actually means in practice, since the term is used loosely in job descriptions.

**Not:** Does not cover the firms/roles operating in these markets (see Participants module).
