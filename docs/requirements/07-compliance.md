# Requirement: Regulations & compliance module

**What:** Cover the regulations a finance data engineer will run into — MiFID II, EMIR, Basel III, GDPR-in-finance, BCBS 239, AML/KYC — with a post-2008 regulatory timeline and, for each, the data engineering implications.

**Why:** This was one of the two explicitly requested focus areas (alongside finance domain knowledge). Regulation is also why a large fraction of finance data pipelines exist (reporting, audit trails, data lineage) — without this module the Pipelines module's "regulatory report generation" content has no grounding.

**Not:** Does not attempt to be legal/compliance training — it's the data engineering implications of each regulation, not full regulatory text.
