# Requirement: Overview module

**What:** A first module that orients a data engineer to how the financial system as a whole fits together — banking, asset management, capital markets — before any instrument- or regulation-specific detail.

**Why:** Without a mental map of "who needs money, who has money, and who sits in between," instrument and regulation modules read as disconnected trivia. The learner needs the big picture first, and needs it tied to data (example schemas per sector) so it connects to their existing DE skillset rather than reading as generic finance-101.

**Not:** Does not cover individual instruments, venues, or regulations — those are separate modules.
