# Requirement: Participants module ("who does what")

**What:** Explain buy side vs sell side, front/middle/back office, and other recurring roles (custodians, CCPs, data vendors).

**Why:** Job descriptions and meetings in finance assume fluency in this cast of characters. A data engineer who doesn't know whether a request is coming from "the front office" or "the back office" will misjudge urgency, data freshness requirements, and who owns a given dataset.

**Not:** Does not cover regulatory bodies (see Compliance module, "Regulators you'll hear about").
