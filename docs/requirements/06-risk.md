# Requirement: Risk management module

**What:** Explain the major risk categories — market, credit, operational, liquidity — and, for each, the specific data it requires to measure/report.

**Why:** Risk reporting is one of the largest sources of data engineering work in finance (regulatory and internal). The learner needs to connect each risk type to concrete data requirements so they can recognize risk-related data requests for what they are.

**Not:** Does not cover the regulations that mandate risk reporting (see Compliance module) — this module is the risk concepts and data themselves.
